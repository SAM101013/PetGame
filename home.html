Welcome to Animal Insync

The Team's Journey

Once upon a coding adventure, Ope, Jack, Sophia, and Syeda embarked on a mission to create an interactive game called Animal Insync. It was a collaborative effort where each team member brought their unique skills to the table, forming a powerhouse of creativity and innovation.

As avid learners and developers, the team decided to harness the power of JavaScript and the Document Object Model (DOM) to breathe life into their digital creatures. The principles of object-oriented programming (OOP) guided design, making the game scalable and maintainable.

Through collab coding sessions, bug-squashing marathons, and moments of sheer inspiration, the team sculpted a fascinating world of dinosaurs and a peacock, each with their own set of intriguing actions. The game offers an interactive experience for young players.The target audience was boys and girls age 3-7 years

Behind the Scenes

Tools like Trello served as the team's compass, navigating the project management landscape. Pixel, the magical wand for creating stunning images, painted the vibrant landscapes of Animal Instincts. When challenges arose, Stack Overflow became the oracle for problem-solving, providing solutions to intricate puzzles.

Google Fonts, Gif images, MDN, and W3Schools joined the ensemble, playing crucial roles in enhancing the overall design and functionality of the game. The development process was a symphony of collaboration, creativity, and a sprinkle of JavaScript magic.
